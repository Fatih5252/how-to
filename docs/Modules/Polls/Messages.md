title: Messages

Here, you type all the Messages that the Polls module uses. You can customize these messages to fit the tone and style of your server, making the polling experience more engaging for your users.

---

**embed**
![image](/img/modules/polls/embed.png)

type: [Embed](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/type/embed)

required: true

Here, you can customize the embed that the bot sends when a poll is created. You can change the title, description, color, and other aspects of the embed to make it more visually appealing and informative for your users.
