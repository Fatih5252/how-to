---
title: Christmas Countdown
---

Here, you can configure the Christmas Eve, Christmas Day and New years Eve countdown. If you select a text channel, Voice channel or a category, the bot will rename these channels / category. it would be renamed, on how many months days and hours are left until Christmas Eve, Christmas Day or New years Eve. unfortuanatly you can only do one, you can't do everything, you have to choose if you want a Christmas Eve, Christmas Day or New years Eve countdown.

---

**Enabled?**
![image](/img/modules/winter-holidays/enabled-countdown.png)

type: [Boolean](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/type/boolean) (AKA True/False)

required: false (true, if you want this feature)

If you don't want this feature, you can leave it off, but if you want a Christmas Eve, Christmas Day or New years Eve countdown turn this on, this activates the Christmas Eve, Christmas Day and New years Eve countdown feature.

---

**Countdown-Channel**

type: [Channel Select](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/type/channel)

required: True (if you enabled the "Enabled?" option)

Here, you have to select the channel or category that the Bot should rename to show how many months days and hours are left until Christmas Eve, Christmas Day or New years Eve.

---

**Countdown until**

type: [options](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/type/options)

required: True (if you enabled the "Enabled?" option)

Here, you have to select if you want a Christmas Eve, Christmas Day or New years Eve countdown, you can only choose one, you can't choose multiple.
