---
title: Messages
---

Here, you only customize the Embed message that the Quiz module sends when a quiz is created. You can customize this message to fit the tone and style of your server, making the quiz experience more engaging for your users.

---

**Embed**
![image](/img/modules/quiz-module/embed.png)

type: [Embed](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/type/embed)

required: true

Here, you can customize the embed that the bot sends when a quiz is created. You can change the title, description, color, and other aspects of the embed to make it more visually appealing and informative for your users. I recommend that you leave everything as it is, but you can change it every time you want.
