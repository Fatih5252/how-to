# 👋 Welcome to HowTo?!

Welcome to How to? your place for all things configuration inside SCNX. This community project is here to show you how to set everything in the Configuration up properly.

Configurations can sometimes feel confusing or difficult, and even small mistakes can lead to big problems. That’s why we created this website: to provide clear guidance that helps you get your settings right the first time.

We don’t just cover modules we also provide configuration examples for custom commands, so you can tailor SCNX exactly to your needs.

This area is updated regularly, so you’ll always find the latest information. If you have any questions, suggestions, or feedback, feel free to connect with us on Discord.

Thank you for being part of the SCNX community!
