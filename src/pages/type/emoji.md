Are you wondering what the "Type" section on this website means?

Here is an explanation:

The "Emoji Select" type means that you have to choose an emoji from a dropdown.  
For example, in the [Applications module](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/docs/modules/applications) (section: Emoji), you have to choose an emoji that will be displayed next to the category in the Discord dropdown menu. It's similar to the [Role Select](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/docs/type/role) type, but instead of roles, you choose emojis.
