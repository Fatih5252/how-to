# Select Role

Are you wondering what the "Type" section on this website means?

here is an explanation:

The "Role Select" type means that you have to choose a role in your server from a dropdown.  
For example, in the [Applications module](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/docs/modules/applications) (section: Roles that can process applications), you have to choose a role that can approve or deny applications. its the same as the [Channel Select](https://howto.scnx-tutorials.de/docs/type/channel) type, but instead of channels, you choose roles.
